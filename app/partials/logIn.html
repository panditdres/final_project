KickApp

No account? No problem, create one here

Fancy playing a pickup game but struggling to find a field?

KickApp allows you to find nearby football fields to play around your neighbourhood. You can see how many people are playing, reviews for each field and even add friends if you meet them and they are users of the app. Each location will indicate who is playing on the day and the type of field it is.

Our Services

Pick Up Game

Find a pick up game nearby and see if your friends are there.

Locations

Easy to locate with markers indicating each field and their respective information.

Create your network

Create events

Invite your friends to play and create a game.
